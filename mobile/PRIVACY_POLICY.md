# Privacy Policy

**Last updated: March 12, 2026**

Jet Lag: Hide & Seek is an unofficial companion app for Jet Lag: The Game. This policy describes what data is collected and how it is used.

## Data Stored on Your Device

All game data — questions, zone selections, and map settings — is stored locally on your device using AsyncStorage. This data never leaves your device and is not transmitted to any server.

If you enter a Thunderforest API key, it is stored locally on your device only.

## Location

The app can optionally access your device's location to display your position on the map. Location is used only within the app and is never transmitted or stored outside your device. You can deny location permission and the app will still function.

## Analytics

The app uses [PostHog](https://posthog.com) to collect anonymous usage analytics (e.g. which question types are used, when zones are added or removed). No personally identifiable information is collected. Analytics can be reviewed at PostHog's [privacy policy](https://posthog.com/privacy).

## Crash Reporting

The app uses [Sentry](https://sentry.io) to collect crash reports and error logs in production builds. These reports may include device type, OS version, and a stack trace. No personally identifiable information is included. Crash reports are reviewed at Sentry's [privacy policy](https://sentry.io/privacy/).

## Third-Party Services

Map tile requests are sent to third-party tile providers (CartoDB, Thunderforest) and OpenStreetMap's Overpass API to load map data. These requests are subject to their respective privacy policies.

## Contact

If you have questions about this policy, please open an issue at [github.com/wztlei/hide-and-seek](https://github.com/wztlei/hide-and-seek/issues).
